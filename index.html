Open Editor

MTLM is an interactable tier list maker using basic JSON. It was designed for making song tier lists as its really hard show people your rankings when you just see album covers. MTLM provides the solution! MTLM lets you add a title, description, image, and link to each item on your tier list. By simply hoving over the image, you can see all the information your viewers need to know.

Tutorial

To make your own Tier List, open the editor with the top-most link. The editor will contain tools that will let you make your tier list. Once you finish creating your tier list, copy the generated link and save it. That link will take you to your finished tier list!
